Who We Are

About us

PT. Agrakom Para Relatika (AgrakomPR) is an Indonesia independent local agency that was established in 1997. We have been trusted to handle blue chip clients in retainer and ad-hoc basis with 10 – 15 clients annually. Our team comes from various educational backgrounds including Communications, Law, Economics, Journalism, Engineering and Agriculture that makes it easier to handle clients with diverse business and interests.

We believe in our work values: Commitment, Creativity, Flexibility, Result and Honesty, everytime we do our job, together with Zest and Enthusiasm for the account as our work spirit. We have a good understanding on local market, network in Java, Sumatera, Kalimantan, Bali, as well as a strong relationship with media across all industries.

Our Services

We provide One Stop Solutions for our various clients with Public Relations, Social Media and Marketing Services to support the program.

Public Relation

As our main core business, we support all round Public Relations activities in media relations: Press Releases – Interviews – Press Briefings – Press Conference – Tele Video Conference – Story Pitch – Media Visit – Technology Updates – Media Seeding/Review – Media Coaching – Media Tracking – Translation
